# TGLabs AI Editorial System

## Project Overview

TGLabs is an AI-assisted gaming news platform focused on:

- mobile gaming
- MMORPG
- gacha games
- SEO-driven content
- AI-assisted editorial workflow

Primary goals:

- SEO traffic growth
- high publish consistency
- scalable AI content pipeline
- monetization through ads

---

## Editorial Direction

Focus heavily on:

- mobile games
- gacha games
- MMORPG
- anniversary events
- collaboration events
- limited banners
- free summons
- global launches
- high-engagement updates

Avoid prioritizing:

- console-only games
- indie PC games
- hardware news
- low-value patch notes
- generic gaming topics

---

## Writing Style

Articles must:

- sound human-written
- sound like a real Thai gaming website
- avoid robotic AI phrasing
- avoid academic tone
- avoid corporate wording
- use practical gamer language
- use short readable paragraphs
- prioritize readability and SEO

---

## Anti-AI Writing Rules

Avoid excessive usage of:

- "นอกจากนี้"
- "อย่างไรก็ตาม"
- "ถือเป็น"
- "กล่าวได้ว่า"
- "ในส่วนของ"
- repetitive transitions
- overly formal tone
- generic filler paragraphs

Do not generate:

- repetitive conclusions
- AI-style summaries
- fake opinions
- generic engagement bait

---

## SEO Rules

Prioritize:

- searchable keywords
- game names
- event names
- update versions
- banner names
- player intent

Titles should:

- be direct
- be readable
- avoid clickbait
- avoid excessive punctuation
- prioritize CTR + search intent
