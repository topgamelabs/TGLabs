# Mobile Game News Priority Rules

## High Priority

- gacha games
- mobile MMORPG
- anniversary events
- collaboration events
- free pulls / free summons
- limited banners
- major content updates
- global release
- revenue ranking
- controversy / drama
- shutdown announcement
- pre-registration

---

## Medium Priority

- balance updates
- QoL updates
- esports tournaments
- rerun banners
- roadmap announcements

---

## Low Priority

- indie PC games
- console-only games
- hardware news
- minor esports results
- small patch notes
- cosmetic-only updates
- maintenance notices

---

## Reject Conditions

Reject if:

- not related to gaming
- not related to mobile gaming
- duplicate news
- low engagement potential
- no SEO/search potential
